About Us

Learn more about NetClaks, our journey, and our vision for the future of automotive excellence.

Who We Are

Founded in 2013, Netclaks has grown into one of the leading car trading companies in Egypt. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation by offering a wide range of renowned international brands and delivering flexible sales solutions tailored to the diverse needs of our customers.

Guided by a customer-first philosophy, we exceeded our highest sales milestone in 2018 and continue to prioritize trust, transparency, and innovation. Our dedicated team ensures that every customer receives the support they need—whether it’s answering inquiries, providing installment plans, or offering financing facilities through Netclaks. More than just a trading company, we are committed to creating a smarter, simpler, and more enjoyable car ownership experience.

Our Vision

At Netclaks, our vision is to be the trusted partner for every driver in choosing their car. We strive to offer a wide range of major brands while making every step of the car-buying journey simple, transparent, and truly enjoyable.

Our Message

At Netclaks, we are here to support every customer with flexible offers and convenient installment systems. Our expert consultants work closely with you to help choose the option that fits your needs best, while we remain committed to delivering exceptional service and an experience you can rely on.

Our Goal

Our goal at Netclaks is to make car ownership both enjoyable and practical. Through our dedicated team, we support you at every step of the journey while offering flexible payment options and installment plans with competitive banking rates—ensuring convenience, trust, and peace of mind in every purchase.
